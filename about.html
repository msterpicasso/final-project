Our Mission

Our mission is to make sure that every camper, whether you are a beginner camper or an advance camper, know the necessary tools and rules to having a great and more importantly a safe camping experience.

Our Beliefs

We believe that everyone should have somewhere to find other campers across the world to communciate with and share their experiences with. Alongside, knowing the tools recommended by our experts and recommended by other campers to make camping worthwhile and to make it memorable! We strive to have 100% accurate and helpful information available 24/7 around the clock.

About the creator

Our founder, Joshua Cox, is a beginner camper himself and wanted to find a way to help other beginner campers like himself. He didn't know where to start or where to look for the different materials and the different advice so he could enjoy his camping experience in the safest way possibe. So, he created this website for the many different people out in the world who probably ended up in the same situation or predicament just like him.
